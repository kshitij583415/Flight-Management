Password Recovery Process (Secret Question)

If you have forgotten your password, you can follow these steps to recover it
 using your secret question and answer:

1. Visit our website at [Insert website URL].
2. Click on the "Forgot Password" link or button on the login page.
3. Enter your registered email address in the provided field.
4. Click on the "Submit" or "Recover Password" button.
5. You will be prompted to answer your secret question.
6. Enter the answer to your secret question in the provided field.
7. If your answer matches the one on record, you will be able to reset your password.

Please note the following:

- It is important to provide the exact answer that you provided during the account setup.
- Answers are case-sensitive, so ensure that you enter your answer correctly.
- If you don't remember the answer to your secret question, please contact our support team
 at [Insert contact information] for further assistance.

Once you have successfully answered the secret question and reset your password, 
you will be able to log in to your account using the new password.

If you have any additional questions or need further assistance,
 please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team.
