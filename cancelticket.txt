Cancelled Ticket Information
---------------------------

If you need to cancel your train ticket, please read the following information regarding the cancellation process:

1. Cancellation Policy:
   - Ticket cancellation is subject to the cancellation policy applicable to the specific train service and ticket type.
   - Different trains and ticket categories may have varying cancellation rules and charges.

2. Ticket Cancellation Methods:
   - Online Cancellation: If you booked your ticket online, you can usually cancel it through the online booking platform or website. Log in to your account and navigate to the ticket cancellation section to initiate the cancellation process.
